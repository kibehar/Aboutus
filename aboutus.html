--About--

     Ethiopia is a diverse country of many cultures and traditions.Nu Gebeya was born with the idea of showing what Ethiopia has to offer to the world. It's striving to be the one place where everything Ethiopian can be found. Nu Gebeya also works with artists, craftsmen, designers, potters, and other talents who identify their work as Ethiopian tradition by giving them a platform to show their works.

    The name ‘Nu Gebeya’ means “come shop here”. It was named Nu Gebeya because for Ethiopians, gebeya is not a place only for buying and selling goods but also for sharing information, building relationships, and socializing. And That's Nu Gebeya. Nu Gebeya features traditional items from Habesha Libs to Baltena products to traditional jewelry all in one place. Our products are carefully chosen to bring our customers the best quality products.
